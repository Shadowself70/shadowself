ShadowDrums

Welcome to ShadowDrums, a web-based drum machine.

  • Start/Stop: Use the START button or press the SPACEBAR.
  • Save/Load: Saves or loads all patterns and settings to your browser's local storage.
  • Pattern Buttons (1-8): Click to switch between 8 different patterns.
  • Swing: Adjusts the swing amount (0-75%).
  • Clear: Clears all steps from the currently selected pattern.
  • Grid Cells:
    • Click 1: ON (Red) - Plays a normal note.
    • Click 2: ACCENT (Bright Red) - Plays a louder note.
    • Click 3: OFF (Dark) - Silences the step.
    • Click + Drag: "Draws" the new state across multiple cells.
  • Track Controls (Per Row):
    • Dropdown Menu: Select one of 4 sounds for that track.
    • P (Pitch): Tunes the sound +/- 1 octave.
    • V (Volume): Controls the volume for that specific track.
    • REVERB: Toggles the reverb effect on or off.
    • DELAY: Toggles the delay effect on or off.
    • M (Mute): Mutes the track.
    • S (Solo): Solos the track.
  • Main Controls:
    • TEMPO: Adjusts the master BPM.
    • MASTER VOL: Adjusts the final output volume.
